===========================================
IMPORTANT NOTES:
===========================================

1. This template uses SIMPLE variable substitution only - no conditionals
2. All variables are always sent from the form, so they will always have values
3. The daysList and timesList are formatted strings that combine all selections
4. Empty fields will show "Not provided" or "None" instead of being blank

===========================================

===========================================

- firstName
- lastName
- email
- phone
- age
- courseType
- course
- experience
- daysList (formatted string of selected days)
- timesList (formatted string of selected time slots)
- goals
- questions
